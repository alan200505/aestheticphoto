WHAT IS MEANT BY AESTHETIC PHOTOGRAHS

Aesthetic photography is a photographic practice of capturing visual beauty within an image. Just as the term “beauty” can be subjective, “aesthetic” can be distinguished as something that is immediately visually pleasing to a viewer.v
